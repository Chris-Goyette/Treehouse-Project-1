Background

As much as I would like to say that I am an aspiring web developer, the truth is I don't know what I want to do yet. One thing I can say with certainty is that I need to level up my skills - especially if I want to make it in the Bay Area.

Outside of trying to figure out my life, I work a fulltime job in the fintech industry as an Investor Services Associate for LendignClub. My past roles have included working as a specialist in credit risk analysis and fraud prevention.

In my most recent role I used SQL and had a small taste of data analysis and because of this, a part of me is saying should pursue learning SQL and Python.

On the other hand, the curious side to me is very interested in web development and the opportunities this skill could bring.

After mulling about this issue for months, I have decided that the only way to figure out what I want to do is to just take action and see where it goes.

Goals

I want to complete the Front-End Web Development Techdegree at Treehouse and master the process of building web sites and increase my knowledge, skills, and abilities in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

For good or for worse, I am committed to document my experiences throughout this new chapter in my life. Thank you for supporting me and following me on my journey. See you on the other side!
